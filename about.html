About Sri Vidyalaya PU College

Sri Vidyalaya Pre-University College, Shivamogga is a reputed institution dedicated to delivering high-quality Commerce education with a strong focus on academic excellence and overall student development. Established in 2014–15 under the guidance of Sri Vidyalaya Educational Trust, the college has built a trusted name among students and parents in Shivamogga.

As the First AI PU Commerce College in Shivamogga, Sri Vidyalaya PU College adopts an AI-first approach to modern education by integrating artificial intelligence into teaching, learning, and academic processes. This innovative approach enables students to develop future-ready skills, analytical thinking, and digital literacy aligned with evolving global education standards.

Our institution provides a disciplined and supportive learning environment where students are encouraged to learn, grow, and achieve their goals. We combine strong academic foundations in core Commerce subjects—Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, and Statistics—with personality development, communication skills, and leadership training to ensure holistic growth.

The college ensures that AI integration enhances the prescribed PU curriculum without deviation, maintaining academic standards while adopting modern, technology-driven teaching methodologies. We are committed to gradually evolving into a digitally empowered campus with AI-supported academic processes that ensure quality education and future readiness.

With a vision to become one of the leading Commerce PU colleges in Shivamogga, we aim to shape confident, responsible, and skilled individuals. Our mission focuses on building strong academic foundations while promoting discipline, ethical values, and career-oriented development, preparing students for professional courses such as CA, CS, Actuarial Science, and higher education in commerce and business.

About Our Courses

Sri Vidyalaya PU College offers specialized Commerce education designed to build a strong academic foundation and prepare students for future professional careers. Our program focuses on core subjects such as Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, and Statistics, ensuring students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical understanding.

We provide structured coaching for SEBA (Statistics, Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy) with a strong emphasis on analytical and quantitative skills. In addition, we guide students toward higher education and professional paths such as CA, CS, B.Com, and BBA.

Special focus is given to emerging and high-potential career fields like Actuarial Science, which involves using mathematics and statistics to analyze risk and make financial decisions. This field requires strong analytical thinking and offers excellent global career opportunities.

Through focused guidance, skill development, and career awareness, we prepare students to confidently pursue advanced studies and succeed in competitive, future-oriented professions.
