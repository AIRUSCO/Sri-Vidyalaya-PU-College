Our Courses

At Sri Vidyalaya PU College, we offer a carefully designed Commerce education that builds a strong academic foundation while preparing students for real-world success. As the First AI PU Commerce College in Shivamogga, our approach combines traditional academic excellence with modern, technology-driven learning.

We focus not only on subjects but also on skills, career readiness, and future opportunities, ensuring students are well-prepared for higher education and professional careers.

Academic and Professional Courses: SEBA and CEBA

The SEBA and CEBA combinations are among the most powerful Commerce streams, designed to build a strong foundation in finance, business, analytics, and technology. While SEBA focuses on core commerce subjects such as Statistics, Economics, Business Studies, and Accountancy, CEBA integrates Computer Science to enhance digital and technical capabilities. These programs go beyond traditional classroom learning by combining conceptual clarity with practical application, enabling students to develop analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and decision-making skills. With the integration of modern teaching methodologies, AI-enabled learning, and real-world exposure, students gain a competitive edge in today’s dynamic and technology-driven economy. Together, SEBA and CEBA prepare students not only for professional courses like Chartered Accountancy (CA), Company Secretary (CS), and Actuarial Science, but also for higher education and diverse career opportunities in finance, business management, entrepreneurship, and emerging digital domains.

PU COMMERCE – SEBA (CORE PROGRAM)

The SEBA combination is one of the most powerful Commerce streams, designed to build a strong base in finance, business, and analytics. It equips students with both theoretical knowledge and practical understanding, making it ideal for students who aim to pursue professional and business-oriented careers.

Subjects Offered:

  • • Statistics – Develops analytical thinking and data interpretation skills
  • • Economics – Builds understanding of financial systems and decision-making
  • • Business Studies – Provides knowledge of business operations and management
  • • Accountancy – Strengthens financial and accounting fundamentals
  • • English – Improves communication, comprehension, and language proficiency
  • • Kannada – Strengthens regional language skills and cultural understanding

Certification & Skill Development Courses

We provide industry-relevant skill training alongside academics, giving students a strong advantage in finance, taxation, accounting, and technology. Our programs emphasize practical learning through real-world applications, hands-on training, and industry-oriented tools, ensuring students are not limited to theoretical knowledge. By integrating modern teaching methodologies, digital platforms, and career-focused training, we help students develop analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and professional competence. This approach enables learners to build both technical expertise and the confidence required to perform effectively in today’s competitive and rapidly evolving professional environment.

  • Accounting & Finance Training:
  • • Tally (Official Certification)
  • • Tally + GST (Practical Training)
  • • GST Filing & Taxation
  • • GST Practical Applications
  • • Accounting Basics & Financial Records

  • Professional Course Foundation:
  • • CA Basics (Introduction to Chartered Accountancy)
  • • CA Foundation Level Preparation

  • Technology & Data Skills:
  • • Microsoft Excel (Basic to Advanced)
  • • Advanced Excel & Data Handling
  • • Google Sheets for real-time collaboration

  • • Zoho Books (Cloud Accounting Software)

  • • Excel Basics
